About us

A community of exclusive 2,3 BHK & Duplex Luxury Apartments

Thoughtfully designed units to give you higher carpet area efficiency.

Elegant Tranquility, brought to you by Elegant Altima, presents a unique opportunity to own a piece of real estate in the vibrant city of Bangalore. Located in the sought-after area of Kengeri, this residential development offers a range of 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartment formats to choose from.

Spread across a sprawling 3.47 acres of prime land, Elegant Tranquility is designed to incorporate modern and contemporary features to elevate your lifestyle. The unit sizes range from a comfortable 771.0 sq. ft. to a spacious 1246.0 sq. ft., catering to different preferences and needs. The project was launched in May 2023 and is slated for possession in September 2029, ensuring you have ample time to plan your move.

About Developer

Elegant Builders & Developers is a real estate company in Bangalore, India that builds residential and commercial projects. Founded in 2004, the company has completed around 18 projects in south Bangalore and is planning to expand to other parts of the city. Elegant Builders is known for its quality, useful detailing, and value for money. They have a focus on timely delivery, superior design, premium amenities, and convenient locations

Known for its commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction, Elegant Builders has delivered numerous successful projects ranging from affordable housing to luxury residences and commercial spaces. The company focuses on leveraging cutting-edge technology and architectural expertise to create developments that cater to the evolving needs of modern urban living.
